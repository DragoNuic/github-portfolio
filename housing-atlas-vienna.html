    About this analysis

    This view maps the distribution and scale of social and municipal housing (Gemeindebau and cooperative stock) across Vienna's districts, tracing how the city's housing model has evolved and where new development is concentrated. Base map and housing footprints are drawn from QGIS-exported GIS layers.
