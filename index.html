Hi, my name is Drago Nuic and I am interested in exploring urban spaces around us, the systems that shape it, and how these spaces in return shape us and our societies.

Featured projects

  • The Vienna Grassroots Atlas

    I am currently working on mapping the grassroots initiatives and organisations that contribute to the social, economic and environmental resilience of the city of Vienna. I believe their work is an an often unseen pillar of upholding the wellbeing of all in the city, and it is my hope that this project will become a platform from which I can highlight these efforts, analyse their contributions, and to critically assess the gaps and challenges that they face.
