Social Housing Atlas

Housing stability is a key pillar of a resilient and inclusive society, on which social wellbeing, civic participation, and the legitimacy of democratic institutions heavily rest on. This project is a collection of essays, maps and reports that analyses the importance of housing as a public good, its development and evolution, and the challenges it faces across various international contexts.
